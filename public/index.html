Sevillian City

Location

Sevilla is located in the southwestern part of the Iberian Peninsula and is the capital of the province of Sevilla. It is located on the banks of the Guadalquivir River, which gives it a picturesque setting.

Typical Food and Drinks

Paella

Its name comes from the container where it is prepared, the frying pan, which in Latin is called patella. So paella in Valencian also means pan, but in Spanish the name of the container is used to name the dish. Paellera is the name given to the utensil used to cook it.

Casetas

What are "casetas"?

Casetas are enclosed areas with fabric walls and roofs. These can be public or private, where people eat what they buy at the fair.

Flamenco

Flamenco is an echo of Andalusian soul.

Flamenco is a deeply rooted musical and artistic genre in Spanish culture, especially in the region of Andalucia, where it has its origins.

Origins.

Flamenco artists typically wear traditional costumes, such as long dresses with ruffles for women and elegant outfits for men. Attire is an integral part of the visual representation of flamenco.

Vestimenta

Flamenco artists typically wear traditional costumes, such as long dresses with ruffles for women and elegant outfits for men. Attire is an integral part of the visual representation of flamenco.

Cultural Significance

Flamenco is considered Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity by UNESCO and has influenced music and art worldwide. It is a symbol of passion and Andalusian cultural identity.
